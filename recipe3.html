Artichoke Lemon Risotto


Ingredients
6 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup minced onion
2 cups arborio rice
6 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
1 can each of artichoke bottoms and hearts, well rinsed and cut into 1/4-inch slices
Zest and juice of 2 ripe lemons
1 tablespoon kosher salt


Directions

1) Stir onions and olive oil in a 14-inch microwaveable dish. Microwave uncovered for 3 minutes.

2) Without removing dish from oven, stir in the rice and cook for 3 minutes

3)Pour in the broth and stir. Cook for 12 more minutes.

4)Stir in the artichoke pieces and cook for 6 more minutes.

5) Stir in the lemon zest and juice; cook for 2 more minutes. *Remove from oven; taste and stir in salt (or add more if broth had no salt).
