omlette


Ingredients
1 large egg
2 tbsp. shredded cheddar cheese
1 tbsp. diced green bell pepper
2 egg whites
Salt and ground black pepper to taste
Cooking spray


Directions

1) Combine egg, egg whites, cheddar cheese, bell pepper, salt, and ground pepper in a microwave-safe mug coated with non-stick spray.

2) Microwave on high for 1 minute; stir.

3) Return to microwave and cook until eggs are completely set, 1 to 1 and 1/2 minutes longer.

4) Sprinkle cheese on top and serve.
