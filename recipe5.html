White Chocolate and Matcha


Ingredients
1 egg
1 slice of butter 1 cm. thick (30g)
6 squares of white chocolate (30g)
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp matcha green tea
5 tbsp. plain flour
2 tbsp. caster sugar


Directions

1) In a mug: melt the butter with the chocolate in the microwave for 30 seconds.

2) Beat the mixture until smooth, then allow it to cool slightly. One by one, whisk in the egg, sugar, flour, baking powder and green tea.

3) Push four raspberries into the middle of the mixture. Cook in the microwave for one minute and 40 secs.

4) Decorate with one raspberry. Allow to cool for one minute before eating.
