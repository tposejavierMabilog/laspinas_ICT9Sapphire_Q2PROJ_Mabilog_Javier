Chocolate Microwave Torte


Ingredients
2 and 1/2 Tbsp. melted butter
2 Tbsp. baking cocoa
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 tsp. vanilla
1 egg white
2 shakes salt
1/8 tsp. baking soda
1 Tbsp. flour


Directions

1) In a microwavable cereal bowl, melt your butter. Add the cocoa, and combine. Add the mix till combined.

2) Add the vanilla, mix again! And add the egg white

3) Sprinkle on the salt, baking soda and flour and mix till combined. sugar,

4) Divide equally into two small ramekins or bowls and microwave 1 at a time for no more than 45 seconds.
