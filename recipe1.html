Chicken Penne al Fresco


Ingredients
4 garlic cloves, peeled
2 cups cherry tomatoes
3 cups uncooked penne pasta
4 cups chicken broth (or 3 cups chicken broth and 1 cup white wine)
1/2 tsp. each salt and coarsely ground black pepper
2 tablespoons dried basil
2 cups diced grilled chicken breasts
2 cups Mozzarella Cheese


Directions

1) Spray Deep Covered Baker (or casserole dish) with olive oil.

2) Mince the garlic and add it to the dish.

3) Add tomatoes. Cover; microwave on HIGH until tomatoes begin to burst, stirring after 2 minutes.

4) Crush tomatoes using the back of a spoon.

5) Add pasta, broth, salt and blackpepper.

6) Return baker (or casserole dish) to microwave; cover and microwave on HIGH until pasta is tender.

7) Carefully remove baker from microwave and remove lid, lifting away from you. Add cheese and chicken to baker; mix well. Add basil and mix well.
